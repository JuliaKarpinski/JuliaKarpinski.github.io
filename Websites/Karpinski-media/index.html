Poltergeist: A Full Summary

Poltergeist Movie Summary

The Freeling Family moves into a home that seems to be normal, but turns out to be haunted by ghosts, as the house sits upon a graveyard. The youngest daughter of the family, Carol Anne, seems to connect with the ghost through a dead channel on the television. Not long after, the ghosts enter the house walls and begin to play seemingly harmless pranks on the family, like moving chairs, breaking plates, and rearranging tables. However, these pranks escalate into something much more deadly, as the ghosts sabotage the family with deadly trees and murderous dolls. Eventually, Carol Anne gets abducted by the ghosts within her own bedroom closet, which acts as the entrance to the supernatural world. With the family alarmed, they hire several parapsychologists to come within their home to investigate the occurrence in attempts to regain possession of their child before it's too late.
