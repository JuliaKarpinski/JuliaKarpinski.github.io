Trip to France

Landmarks and places to visit in France

When most people think of france, they think of the famous landmark, the Eiffel Tower. This symbol of Paris is a hot tourist attraction that brings in many from other countries to visit. Within France, there are also beautiful museums like the Louvre Museum, which was a palace that used to be a royal residence. The Palace of Versailles is also a place to tour in France, which became Louis XIV's symbol of absolute power, which features the Hall of Mirrors within the castle.

Why I want to visit France

I want to visit France because I want to experience the culture of the French. I also want to be able to visit the Eiffel Tower as well. Although, France is not the first place I would go to if I were to visit Europe any time soon.
