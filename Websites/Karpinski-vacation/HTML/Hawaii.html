Trip to Hawaii

Landmarks and places to visit in Hawaii

The beautiful beaches in Hawaii is definitely a reason alone to travel to this tropical island. Waikiki Beach and Diamond Head State Monument is definitely included in among the best beaches to visit in Hawaii. Another beach to visit is within the Hanauma Bay Nature Preserve, which is a protected bay that formed within the crater of a volcano. It is also popular for snorkeling.

Why I want to visit Hawaii

I want to visit Hawaii because this island is what you imagine when you simply think of the word vacation. The island seems like paradise to me and I wish to be there to physically experience the island.
