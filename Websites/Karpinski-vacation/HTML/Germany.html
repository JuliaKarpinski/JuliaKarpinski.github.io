Trip to Germany

Landmarks and places to visit in Germany

Within the stunning country of Germany, a popular tourist destination includes Berlin's Brandenburg Gate. As Berlin's most recognizable structure, the gate stands 26 meters high with a chariot and horses perched on top of the gate itself. Another beautiful place within Germany includes Neuschwanstein, a beautiful castle atop a mountain which outlooks the beautiful land behind with valleys, rivers, and towns.
